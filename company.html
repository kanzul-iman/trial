Why we built ORIGAMI?

SharePoint customers often think they have two options when it comes to customizing their SharePoint:

  • taking what Microsoft gives your out-of-the-box
  • building a bespoke site either in-house or with consultants

These options are flawed because they turn leaders’ attention away from growing the organization and into technical tasks.

Though technical tasks are important, they do not guarantee user adoption—the ultimate goal for an intranet.
